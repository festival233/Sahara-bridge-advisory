Verification workflow

A clear process signals professionalism and reduces anxiety for the client.

Intake the materials

We review the counterparties, documents, claims, jurisdictions, and the exact point where trust begins to wobble.

Run the checks

We assess consistency across business identity, documentation, commercial logic, contact behavior, and operating reality.

Flag what matters

We surface contradictions, missing pieces, reputational concerns, timeline issues, and signs that the opportunity needs a different structure.

Advise next move

Proceed, pause, renegotiate, verify deeper, or walk. Sometimes the most profitable move is avoiding a disaster in elegant clothing.
